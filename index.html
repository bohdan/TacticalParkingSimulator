Training Manual
Goal — Park the car inside the green zone.

Plan moves — Drag on the canvas: the ghost car follows your finger along a drivable arc. Use the sliders for fine control. Tap Add move (or double-tap the canvas) to queue it up.

Turn strip — The row of chips above the sliders lists every move; the gold one is the move you're editing. Tap a chip (or a numbered badge on the canvas) to edit that move — changes apply live and later moves re-base automatically, no save needed. Tap ＋ (or another chip) to start/return to a new move; 🗑 Delete removes the selected move.

Run — Animates your full plan; any pending move is auto-committed first. Each move is locked to the exact distance the car actually travels, so the saved plan is precisely what you saw.

★ Stars (par) — like golf, against the level's Par (target move count):
 ★★★ — at or under Par
 ★★☆ — Par + 1 (bogey)
 ★☆☆ — parked

💡 Solution — Loads a known solution for the level. Using it disables leaderboard submission until you press Reset.
