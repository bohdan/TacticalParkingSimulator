How to Play
Goal — Park the car inside the green zone.

Plan moves — Set a steering angle and distance with the sliders (or drag on the canvas), then tap Add move. Moves are queued up as a plan.

Run — Animates your full plan. The last pending move is auto-committed when you hit Run.

Edit past moves — Tap a numbered badge on the canvas to load that move back into the sliders. Update #N saves the change; Cancel discards it.

★ Stars
 ★★★ — Perfect (meets 3-star threshold)
 ★★☆ — Good
 ★☆☆ — Parked, but can do better

Precise mode — Stars based on fewest moves (or shortest distance for Dead End).

Quick mode — Stars based on estimated time: car accelerates/brakes at 2 m/s² up to 3 m/s top speed, steering 60°/s, +1.5s per direction reversal. Short moves are penalised heavily because you can’t build up speed.

💡 Solution — Loads the verified optimal move sequence. Use it as a hint or to see what perfect looks like.
