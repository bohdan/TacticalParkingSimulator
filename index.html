How to Play
Goal — Park the car inside the green zone.

Plan moves — Drag on the canvas: the ghost car follows your finger along a drivable arc. Use the sliders for fine control. Tap Add move (or double-tap the canvas) to queue it up.

Run — Animates your full plan. The last pending move is auto-committed when you hit Run.

Edit past moves — Tap a numbered badge on the canvas to load that move back into the sliders. Update #N saves the change; Cancel discards it.

★ Stars — based on estimated time (car accelerates/brakes at 2 m/s² up to 3 m/s, steering 60°/s, +1.5s per direction reversal):
 ★★★ — Perfect
 ★★☆ — Good
 ★☆☆ — Parked, keep improving

💡 Solution — Loads the verified optimal move sequence. Using it disables leaderboard submission for the level until you press Reset.
